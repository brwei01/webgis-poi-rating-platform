Create Asset

To create an asset, please follow these steps:

  1. load the app
  2. adjust the window to size 'large' (992 to 1200 px)
  3. Click the location you would like to create your asset at
  4. Type in the name of your asset after "Asset Name"
  5. Select the installation date of your asset on the calendar after "installation date"
  6. The coordinates of the location you clicked at should be automatically filled up by the app , however can be changed as you like by input
  7. Click on the "Start Data Upload" button
  8. Click 'ok' through all the prompts
  9. Assets Uploaded

Delete Asset

Here you will find information on how to delete asset.

  1. Log into the database (pgadmin)
  2. Find the asset_id of the asset you would like to delete
  3. Click anywhere on the map and pop up the asset create form
  4. Type in the asset_id you would like to delete after the "Delete ID: "
  5. Click "Delete Single Asset" Button

Failed to save new asset

Possible Reasons may include

  1. The asset name has already been uploaded to the database.
  2. The asset name section or installation date are left blank
  3. The coordinates of the asset are left blank and the app is not able to save asset or create marker
  4. Failed to connect to the database

You may try these options:

  1. Try a different asset name.
  2. Select a correct installation date.
  3. Check your connection (e.g. internet connection and connection to the database)

Trouble finding correct windowsize 'lg' (992 to 1200 px)

You may try these options:

  1. Resize the window untill you can see 2 buttons in the navbar: "Show List of Assets in Best Condition"; "Daily Reporting Rates Graph - All Users"
  2. Open the 'Developer tool on your browser and check the window size'
